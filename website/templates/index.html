Some common questions
were often asked

What is the main purpose of this web application?

The main purpose is to allow users to upload images of plant leaves and receive predictions about potential diseases affecting the leaf.

What kinds of leaf problems can it detect?

The system can identify if a leaf is disease-free, has leaf rust, or leaf spot.

Do I need any special knowledge to use this?

No, you don't need any special knowledge. The website is designed to be user-friendly for anyone interested in plant health.

Are there any tips for taking a good leaf photo?

While not specified, generally it's best to take a clear, well-lit photo of the entire leaf, including any spots or discoloration you're concerned about.

What should I do if the website says my plant has a disease?

The website provides a diagnosis, but for treatment, it's best to consult with a local gardening expert or agricultural extension service.
